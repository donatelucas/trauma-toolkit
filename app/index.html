Trauma Toolkit is a website developed to help victims of traumatizing experiences find peace and shelter. In a world that stigmatizes sexual assault victims, a place of haven can be a way to find help both mentally and physically. We aim to provide victims of these events with the tools and resources to find help and receive any aid necessary to help them continue their day to day lives. While we understand that we cannot solve the issue, we hope to bring continual awareness to the fact that sexual assault happens daily, and we can do our best to provide assistance.

Currently, we cover the subjects of sexual abuse and rape. While they may seem the same, diving into our content shows just how nuanced these subjects can be. Our eventual aim is to provide resources and assistance to those that face all traumatic events, not just the two listed above.

If you have come here not as a victim but as an ally, please review the information to provide assistance to those around you that may have experienced a traumatic event so that they can find help.

In case of emergency, call 911.
